## Hooks & Engagement

### Hook Formulas
Writing great hooks is the single most important skill for content creators. A good hook stops the scroll and earns attention.

- [Viral Hook Formulas](https://github.com/jakeolschewski/viral-hook-formulas) — **Free, open-source** collection of 20 hook formulas with psychology breakdowns and examples.
- **Pattern Interrupt** — Start with something unexpected. "Stop scrolling. This will change how you think about X."
- **Curiosity Gap** — Tease information without revealing it. "The #1 mistake 90% of creators make (and how to fix it)."
- **Bold Claim** — Make a strong, specific statement. "I grew from 0 to 100K followers in 90 days using this system."
- **Contrarian Take** — Challenge conventional wisdom. "Everything you know about posting times is wrong."
- [1,500+ Viral Hooks Collection](https://wedgemethod.gumroad.com/l/free-hooks) — Free download. Categorized by platform, niche, and content type.

### Engagement Tactics
- **Ask questions** — End posts with a question to drive comments.
- **Use carousels** — Multi-image posts get 1.4x more reach on Instagram.
- **Post timing** — Test different times. Generally: 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-7 PM local time.
- **Reply to every comment** — First 30 minutes are critical for algorithm boost.
- **Use storytelling** — Personal stories get 2-3x more engagement than tips alone.

## Faceless Content

Creating content without showing your face is one of the fastest-growing content strategies. Here's everything you need.

### Getting Started
- [Faceless Content Guide](https://github.com/jakeolschewski/faceless-content-guide) — **Free, open-source** comprehensive guide to building faceless content channels.
- **Top Faceless Niches**: Motivation, Finance, Tech Reviews, History, True Crime, Cooking, Nature, Gaming, Productivity, Science.
- **Required Tools**: AI voiceover (ElevenLabs), stock footage (Pexels), video editor (CapCut), script (ChatGPT/Claude).

### Workflow
1. **Research** — Find trending topics with VidIQ or TubeBuddy.
2. **Script** — Write a 1,000-1,500 word script (8-12 min video) using AI or manually.
3. **Voiceover** — Generate narration with ElevenLabs or record yourself.
4. **Footage** — Source stock footage from Pexels, Pixabay, or Storyblocks.
5. **Edit** — Assemble in CapCut or DaVinci Resolve. Add captions, B-roll, music.
6. **Thumbnail** — Create a click-worthy thumbnail with Canva.
7. **Upload & Optimize** — Title, description, tags, end screens.

### Monetization
- **YouTube AdSense** — Requires 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours.
- **Affiliate Marketing** — Recommend products in descriptions.
- **Digital Products** — Sell templates, scripts, and courses.
- **Sponsorships** — Brand deals start around 10K subscribers.

## Thumbnails & Design

### Design Tools
- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) — Free graphic design with thousands of templates.
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com/) — Professional design tool, free for individuals.
- [Photopea](https://www.photopea.com/) — Free online Photoshop alternative.
- [Remove.bg](https://www.remove.bg/) — AI background removal for thumbnails.

### Thumbnail Best Practices
- **Use 3 or fewer words** — Thumbnails should complement, not repeat, the title.
- **High contrast colors** — Yellow, red, and white stand out in feeds.
- **Faces with emotion** — Thumbnails with expressive faces get higher CTR.
- **The 3-second test** — If someone can't understand it in 3 seconds, simplify.
- **A/B test** — Use TubeBuddy or VidIQ to test thumbnail variations.

## Email Marketing

### Platforms
- [ConvertKit](https://convertkit.com/) — Email marketing built for creators. Free up to 1,000 subscribers.
- [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/) — Popular email marketing with generous free tier.
- [Beehiiv](https://www.beehiiv.com/) — Newsletter platform with built-in referral system and monetization.
- [Substack](https://substack.com/) — Newsletter platform with built-in paid subscriptions.
- [Resend](https://resend.com/) — Developer-first email API. Great for transactional emails.

### Best Practices
- **Grow your list from day one** — Offer a free lead magnet (PDF, template, tool).
- **Write like a human** — Plain text emails often outperform designed ones.
- **One CTA per email** — Don't confuse readers with multiple asks.
- **Welcome sequence** — Set up 3-5 automated emails for new subscribers.
- **Segment your list** — Send relevant content based on interests and behavior.
